The Holy Quran

The Quran is the holy book of Islam, believed by Muslims to be the literal word of God (Allah), as revealed to the Prophet Muhammad over a period of 23 years. It is regarded as the final and most complete revelation from God, following earlier scriptures like the Torah and the Bible.

It provides guidance on faith, worship, and conduct, addressing theological, moral, and legal matters. The Quran emphasizes monotheism, justice, and personal spirituality, making it the cornerstone of Islamic life and a source of peace for millions worldwide.
